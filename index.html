The Property

Refined living
on the ridge

Perched at the crest of a quiet cul-de-sac in one of Leesburg's most sought-after communities, 42 Harlow Ridge Court is a study in thoughtful design. Soaring ceilings, wide-plank white oak floors, and an abundance of natural light characterize every room.

The open-plan kitchen and great room spill onto a covered rear terrace overlooking protected woodland — a seamless indoor-outdoor flow perfect for entertaining. The primary suite occupies its own private wing, offering a spa-caliber bath, dual walk-in closets, and a private sitting room.

A finished lower level with walkout access, a dedicated home office, and a three-car garage complete this exceptional offering. Less than five minutes to historic downtown Leesburg and within the highly regarded Loudoun County school district.
